# Compensation & Benefits Panel

## Panel Composition

| Expert | Role | Primary Lens |
|---|---|---|
| **Veronica Pemberton** | Head of Total Rewards | Comp philosophy, market positioning, internal equity |
| **Dr. Aman Bhattacharya** | Equity & Long-Term Incentives Strategist | Equity-pool math, refresh discipline, dilution / retention tradeoff |
| **Sophia Andersen** | Benefits Program Lead | Benefits design, utilization analytics, cost / retention tradeoff |
| **Jeremiah Coleman** | Pay-Equity Analyst | Equity audit methodology, regression discipline, remediation rigor |

See [expert-roster.md](../expert-roster.md) for full persona definitions.

## When to Invoke

- Annual comp cycle prep
- Pay-band refresh or repricing
- Equity-program redesign
- Benefits-program renewal or major plan change
- Pay-equity audit (proactive or regulatory-triggered)
- Pre-IPO comp / equity overhaul

## Review Protocol

### Step 1: CONVENE — Select Active Experts

| Artifact Type | Active Experts |
|---|---|
| Annual comp cycle | All 4 |
| Pay-band refresh | Veronica, Jeremiah, Sophia |
| Equity-program redesign | Aman, Veronica, Jeremiah |
| Benefits review | Sophia, Veronica, Aman |
| Pay-equity audit | Jeremiah, Veronica, Aman |

### Step 2: BRIEF — Present the Artifact

```
## Comp & Benefits Review Target
- **Subject:** [comp philosophy / band / equity / benefits / audit]
- **Population:** [headcount, level mix, geo mix]
- **Market Reference:** [survey set, target percentile]
- **Headline Asks:** [budget, dilution, benefits cost]
- **Constraints:** [budget, equity-pool math, regulatory, board commitments]
- **Recent Changes:** [last cycle, mid-year actions]
```

### Step 3: SOLO — Independent Expert Reviews

**Veronica Pemberton (Head of Total Rewards) reviews:**
- Comp philosophy clarity: target percentile, market posture, mix
- Internal equity discipline; level / role consistency
- Compa-ratio distribution; outlier patterns
- Promotion-comp logic; cliff vs glide
- Manager comp decision-rights and rubric quality
- "Pull a level. Show me the comp distribution. Now show me the performance distribution. Are they coherent?"

**Dr. Aman Bhattacharya (Equity & LTI Strategist) reviews:**
- Equity-pool runway and burn-rate honesty
- New-hire grant guidelines vs market and dilution math
- Refresh strategy: cliff-anchored, performance-tied, level-driven, hybrid
- Retention modeling on outstanding grants
- Plan-design clarity (RSUs vs options, vesting, post-termination exercise)
- "Show me the next 24 months of grant projections. Where does the pool break, and what changes when it does?"

**Sophia Andersen (Benefits Program Lead) reviews:**
- Benefits-design fit to population needs (life stage, geo, role)
- Utilization analytics: are people using what we pay for?
- Healthcare cost trend and renewal posture
- Wellbeing / mental health / leave program adequacy
- Globalization complexity: program parity vs local appropriateness
- "Pick the three highest-cost benefits. Show me utilization and the perceived-value survey data."

**Jeremiah Coleman (Pay-Equity Analyst) reviews:**
- Audit methodology: cohort definition, model specification, controls
- Regression rigor and statistical-significance discipline
- Pattern detection: gender, race, age, geography, tenure
- Remediation logic: triage, magnitude, and pace
- Recurring-audit cadence and prevention-vs-remediation posture
- "Walk me through one cohort's regression. Which controls earned their place, and which protected the result we wanted?"

### Step 4: CHALLENGE

1. **Veronica vs Aman:** "Comp budget is the constraint" vs "Equity refresh is the constraint; cash is downstream"
2. **Sophia vs Veronica:** "Add this benefit" vs "The current benefit utilization says people don't want what we have, let alone more"
3. **Jeremiah vs Veronica:** "Bands are healthy" vs "Within-band variance has a pattern; that's a remediation conversation"
4. **Aman vs Sophia:** "Spend the dollar on equity" vs "Spend the dollar on benefits — at this level the marginal retention is in childcare, not options"
